The Best Movies According to Hannah

My top 4 movies of all time.

1. Howl's Moving Castle

This is my far my favourite animate movie of all time. I love the sweeping views, brilliant animation and the plot loops are fantastic.

2. Bram Stoker's Dracula

While I am absolutely terrified of anything even remotely horror based, I loved this film so much.
The visiting of Romanian legends, and the depiction of love between Winona Ryder and Gary Oldman was something to behold.

3. Gone with the Wind

While this film has many critiques, I believe that this film is a very important piece of history.
It is a painful yet neccessary reminder of humanities greed and arrogance over the wellbeing and lives of the slaves which should never be repeated.

4. Titanic

As someone with a father who has a deep love for all history, I was sucked into this historic tragedy very early on in my life.
As an adult, I still have a hard time watching the large amounts of loss all in just a few hours
